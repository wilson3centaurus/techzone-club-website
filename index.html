About Us

At TechZone Club, we are dedicated to fostering a passionate community of technology enthusiasts, innovators, and learners. Our club serves as a hub for individuals who share a common interest in exploring and embracing the ever-evolving world of technology. With scores of students joining MSUAS every year, TechZone Club aims to organize events that encourage students to connect and socialize, to boost technical and academic interest through workshops and events, and to provide a safe environment for academic learning and growth.

In addition to our weekly meetups (venue to be announced in our WhatsApp group), we organize a variety of events throughout the semester. Our primary focus lies in tech innovation projects, including software development, IoT, and various other domains. We host seminars, tech events, participate in local and national events such as hackathons and the Presidential Innovation Fair, and occasionally secure victories.

Wanna know more about us, our Vision, our Misson, Core Values or How we operate, feel free to download our club handbook here!
